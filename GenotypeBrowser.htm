Welcome to Pf PopGen [@releasenr]

Explore the latest population-level analyses from the MalariaGEN [@organismshort] Population Genomics Community Project, which is working to establish a valuable resource of information on genome variation in [@Organismshort] parasite populations around the world.

Underpinning this work is an active community of investigators undertaking independent research studies to address unique questions about malaria biology and disease in malaria endemic areas. Every study is different but what they all have in common is a genomic component whereby they are partnering to carry out genome sequencing on parasite samples and sharing the population genetic data through this Community Project.

The parasite sequence data used in this analysis was generated by the Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute. This web application was developed by the MRC Centre for Genomics and Global Health.
